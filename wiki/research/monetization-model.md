# Monetization Model

Last updated: 2026-05-17
Source: `Requirement Docs/Expense-Splitting Apps_ Market Opportunity Analysis for a Splitwise Alternative.pdf`

---

## Core Principle

**Never paywall the act of adding an expense.** That is what shattered Splitwise's goodwill. Paywall convenience and analytics only.

---

## Recommended Pricing Tiers

| Tier | India (INR) | Global (USD) |
|------|------------|--------------|
| Free | Unlimited expenses, equal/unequal splits, multi-currency, 1 OCR scan/week, basic CSV export | Same |
| Pro Monthly | ₹49 | $2.99 |
| Pro Annual | ₹299 | $19.99 |
| Pro Lifetime | ₹1,499 | $39.99 |
| Trip Pass (30 days, splittable) | ₹99 | $4.99 |

Pricing is significantly below Splitwise India (₹149/mo or ₹999/yr). The perceived "fair zone" from DesiDime research is ₹300–500/yr or one-time ~₹500.

---

## What Pro Unlocks

- Unlimited receipt OCR + AI item-level scan
- Recurring expenses with smart reminders
- Bank/SMS auto-import
- Analytics charts (category/group/time breakdowns)
- Group admin controls + read-only member access
- JSON backup
- Cross-currency analytics
- Priority support

---

## What Must Stay Free Forever

- Adding expenses (unlimited, no cap, no cooldown)
- All split types (equal, unequal, percentage, shares, itemized)
- Multi-payer per expense
- Multi-currency entry
- Debt simplification
- Offline use
- CSV export
- Group joining via link (no account required for invitees)
- Dark mode

---

## What NOT to Do (Lessons from Splitwise)

- Never add a daily expense cap or cooldown to free tier
- Never paywall currency conversion — users expect it free (every competitor offers it free)
- Never offer annual-only pricing — always offer monthly + lifetime
- Never add ads to a fintech-trust app without warning
- Never silently change free-tier terms — communicate via in-app notice + email
- Never require email/phone to add a friend
- Never refuse community-requested features without offering an alternative path

---

## Viral Mechanic Tied to Monetization

- Trip Pass purchasable as a group expense (Settle Up's "Group Premium" model) — splits the cost among members, reducing per-person friction
- Referral: invite 5 friends → unlock Pro free for 6 months
